Who We Are

Ethereum Waterloo is a project started by 3 UWaterloo students to help foster a community of Ðapp (distributed-app) developers in KW. By running biweekly sessions on campus, we hope to make it as easy as possible for students to begin building on top of the Ethereum Blockchain.
